Section intro block — mono eyebrow, Archivo display title, muted subtitle. Opens every marketing section.

```jsx
<SectionHeader
  eyebrow="// Bidang Usaha"
  title={<>What We <em>Engineer</em></>}
  subtitle="Mechanical & electrical construction, fabrication and installation for heavy industry."
  align="center"
/>
```

Wrap part of the title in `<em>` to tint it brand-blue. Use `align="center"` for centered sections and `onDark` over navy/photo backgrounds.
